Kerb Stones & More

We supply precast concrete kerb stones for road edges and channels, RCC tree guards for roadside and landscape planting, garden benches for parks and public spaces, and other customized precast elements to suit your project.

Kerb stones

  • Typical dimensions: Height 100–250 mm, width 150–300 mm, length 600–1,200 mm.
  • Profiles: Rectangular (low traffic), half-battered and quadrant (high traffic), with drainage profiles where required.
  • Finish: Smooth for pedestrian areas; textured or exposed aggregate for skid resistance.
  • Used to define pavement edge, channel runoff, and protect verges. Installed on prepared foundation with proper bedding and joints.

RCC tree guards

Reinforced concrete tree guards protect young trees from damage, support straight growth, and define planting pits in streets, parks, and medians. Available in standard and custom sizes.

Garden benches

Precast concrete benches for parks, gardens, bus stops, and public areas. Durable, low maintenance, and can be supplied in standard or custom designs.

Customized products

We can manufacture precast concrete elements to your specifications—special profiles, dimensions, finishes, or applications. Contact us with your requirements for a quote.

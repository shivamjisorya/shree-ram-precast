About Us

We manufacture and supply quality precast concrete products for infrastructure and construction.

Shree Ram Precast Concrete Pipes is based in Rohtak, Haryana. We specialise in RCC Hume pipes, manhole systems, SFRC manhole covers, cement paver blocks, concrete road barriers, kerb stones, RCC tree guards, garden benches, and custom precast solutions.

Our products are used in drainage, sewerage, roads, and landscaping projects. We serve government bodies, contractors, and developers across North India.
